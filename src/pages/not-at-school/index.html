Not a School

Technology is transforming our lives at an amazing rate. So we created Not a School to prepare for such an unpredictable future.

Not a School is a week long programme, led by London legend Charlie Dark. Housed in #SamsungKX at London’s Coal Drops Yard, it explores issues that matter to young people. It offers a mix of educational workshops, debates and hands-on projects. Led by some brilliant speakers and mentors, each session is designed to help you gain the practical skills to build yourself, your community, the world and a brilliant future.

So whatever you think about your future, unthink it.

Course Structure

Good vibes only

October 7th – 11th

How do we make the world more compassionate and online more kind?

In a world full of trolls and online negativity, we deep dive into how we can solve the world’s biggest problems with compassion, kindness and empathy.

Key Speakers

Make Sustainability Personal

How can each of us tackle the climate crisis?

October 14th - 18th

Become the next Greta Thunburg. We look at the environmental issues that are shaping the global conversation and challenge in a mini brief to come up with the next big creative idea to reuse tech packaging.

Humanise Technology

How can humans and technology work together to improve everyone’s quality of life?

October 21st - 25th

AI is the wave of the future. We look at all the exciting opportunities that artificial intelligence offers and how it will create a bright future.

Build an Inclusive London

How can we make London a city for everyone?

October 28th – November 1st

Whether it’s by wealth, opportunity or by space, London like many cities is split by a lot of differences. We explore how we can make our buzzing city a place for everyone to live and thrive, and how technology can play a role is making a change for the better.
