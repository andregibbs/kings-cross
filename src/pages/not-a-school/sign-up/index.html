NOT A SCHOOL

Think the unthinkable

What will your future be like? Technology is transforming how we all learn, work, communicate, relax… at an amazing rate. Exciting times. But it makes predicting tomorrow pretty much impossible.

That’s why Samsung created Not a School. By sharing our knowledge, we hope to give you – the next generation of innovators – a head start. Housed in #SamsungKX at London’s Coal Drops Yard, Not a School explores issues that young people told us that matter to them the most.

Build yourself – your community and the world – a brilliant future.
